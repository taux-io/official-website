AEO 指南

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing your website to be the best answer for a user's query. This is different from SEO, which is focused on ranking for keywords.

AEO is about providing the best possible answer to a user's question, and it's becoming more and more important as search engines get better at understanding natural language.

Here are some tips for optimizing your website for AEO:

  • Use natural language in your content.
  • Answer questions directly.
  • Use structured data to help search engines understand your content.
  • Make sure your website is fast and mobile-friendly.
